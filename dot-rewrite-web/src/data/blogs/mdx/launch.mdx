# Welcome to .note

If you're reading this, you're probably looking to transform the way you study and learn. .note is designed to help you do just that by turning your lecture notes, slides, and PDFs into a personalized AI tutor that adapts to your learning style.

## Setting Up Your Account

Getting started with .note is simple:

1. **Sign up for an account** - Head to the homepage and click "Get Started"
2. **Choose your plan** - Select the plan that fits your needs (Free, Verified Student, or Pro)
3. **Complete your profile** - Tell us a bit about your academic focus to help personalize your experience

## Uploading Your First Materials

Once your account is set up, it's time to start building your knowledge base:

// Example of how .note processes your uploads
